About FinTrack+

FinTrack+ is a smart personal finance management web application designed to help individuals take control of their financial lives. Built with Django and modern web technologies, FinTrack+ makes it simple and intuitive to:

  • 💸 Track your income and expenses effortlessly
  • 📊 Visualize your monthly budgets through charts
  • 📅 Manage recurring transactions automatically
  • 🔔 Receive alerts when your spending exceeds set limits
  • 🧾 Export financial summaries in PDF or CSV format

Why FinTrack+?

Whether you're a student, professional, or business owner, FinTrack+ empowers you to make better financial decisions. Our mission is to promote financial literacy and budgeting habits through an easy-to-use, responsive platform available from any device.

Technologies Used

FinTrack+ is built using:

  • Django 5 (Backend)
  • HTML5, Bootstrap 5 RTL (Frontend)
  • Django Allauth (Authentication & Email Verification)
  • Cloudinary (Image Hosting), PostgreSQL (Database)
